Surrounded by three hills – Mankunnu, Kodayathoormala and Thonippara – Elaveezha Poonchira is a must see tourist place in Kottayam. Known for its magnificent views and lush greenery, Elaveezha Poonchira is dotted with exquisite waterfalls and secret alcovesIt is famous among trekkers for its terrain and is the perfect picnic spot,hardly an hour away from the main Kottayam town.
